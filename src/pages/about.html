About The Pines

Good food, great views, and a place to come back to

Who We Are

The Pines Bar & Grill is a hidden gem at Farmington Hills Golf Club, serving golfers, families, and locals throughout the season. Classic American bar and grill fare that's made us a community favorite.

Burger after 18 holes, family dinner, or private event overlooking the course — The Pines is your spot.

Our Kitchen

Recipes made from scratch with real ingredients. American classics done well — hand-formed burgers, steaks to order, fresh-cut fries. Nothing frozen, nothing phoned in.

Restaurant Interior / Staff Photo

The Setting

Farmington Hills Golf Club is a public course that's been part of the community for decades. The Pines sits in the clubhouse with windows over the greens. In summer, the patio is the best seat in the house.

You don't need to golf to eat here. Everyone's welcome.
